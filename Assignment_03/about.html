Introduction:

The present age is called the age of information. Information is considered the most critical factor in the development of a nation. Hence, information technology can be the key to making Bangladesh a developed country.

More Abour Information Technology(IT)

Information Technology (IT) is the technology or devices related to the collection, processing, arrangement, management, and transfer of information. This technology uses computers, the internet, telephone system, and satellite as a global system. The importance of IT is immense in the context official the present modern world. Now information is considered the key factor for the development of a country. Commerce and industries rely on information for making their production and sales plans. International trade also largely depends on information. Information regarding the demand and supply of certain products in different parts of the world may determine the export and import of those products in those areas. The globalization process has progressed much further. Now we can identify ourselves as a part of the whole global village. The difference between nations is getting narrower day by day. We can use the prospect of this globalization only through information technology.
